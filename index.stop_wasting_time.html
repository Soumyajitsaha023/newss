Wasting Time

when we have some work to do which is very inportant we always say to ourselfs that we will do it later or tomorrow which never comes, This is known as procrastination. This is how we waste most of our time.

What is Procrastination?

It is the act of delaying or postponing a task or set of tasks. So, whether you refer to it as Procrastination or akrasia or something else, it is the force that prevents you from following through on what you set out to do.
